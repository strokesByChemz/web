Strokes by Chemz

A unique combination of

letters and watercolours!

"When inky letters meet soft watercolors, something unique blossoms. Etched in parchment and framed with green foliage, they pop out before your eyes, luring your attention to its details."

Hi, I'm Sumysa, the artist behind Strokes. I found my passion in lettering and painting a few years ago, out of sheer boredom. It was just for fun at first; but as time went on, I discovered that this was a serious passion for me, and I started Strokes by Chemz (that's my nickname, if you're curious!)

Letters and names are my forte, but writing out quotes and trying out unusual mediums - such as eggshells and wood slivers - also appeal to my imagination. I have given a few custom wedding card orders a fling before - each hand-painted and lettered to careful perfection, wrapped in vellum and sealed in wax. Not a large order - a couple of hundreds, you see, but immensely satisfying...

Come see my works... Or ping me if you would like to gift a framed name to a beloved someone...
